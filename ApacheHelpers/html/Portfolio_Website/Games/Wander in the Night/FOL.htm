The Haunted Mansion

You are having a nightmarish dream of wandering into the woods. You stumble upon a seemingly haunted Mansion, coming from inside is a red glowing light. You curiously go inside only to found a note:
The only way to get out is to found all missing items to escape and defeat the ghoul. The items are hidden in every corners of the Mansion. The only way to escape is to find all the items before the ghoul gets you to end your life.
